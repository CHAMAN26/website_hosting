About US

Founded in 1986 by Karl Neubecker in Germany, the PHYTEC group remains a majority family-owned enterprise with minority ownership shares held by the managers of our divisions in Germany, North America, France, India and China. With no debt or external financing, PHYTEC has funded its continued growth and expanding international presence entirely through operations. This includes the new PHYTEC Technologie Campus, which we inaugurate in Mainz, Germany in early 2022.
